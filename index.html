Is Frontend Mentor free?
Yes, Frontend Mentor offers both free and premium challenges. The free version provides a good selection of projects, but the premium version includes additional features like design files (Figma), access to advanced challenges, and extra resources.
Can I use Frontend Mentor projects in my portfolio?
Yes! You can use the projects you complete on Frontend Mentor in your portfolio. In fact, it's encouraged because it helps showcase your frontend skills to potential employers or clients.
How can I get help if I'm stuck on a challenge?
There are several ways to get help:
✅ Check the official Frontend Mentor Discord community where developers share tips.
✅ Look at the Frontend Mentor discussions for similar questions.
✅ Search online for solutions related to your issue (HTML, CSS, JavaScript).
✅ Ask for feedback after submitting your project.
